A Space for You

4 Changes that Happen for Clients in Our Work:

 

1) Trust

 

They trust themselves! They notice their initial response and feel comfort in making decisions for themselves while prioritizing their needs.

 

2) Feeling Connected and Present

 

They still have the memories and know what happened but without that big jolt to the system every time something related to the past comes up.The feeling of on edge, hypervigilance and tension in the body changes to being more grounded in the present and regulated.

 

3) The World Gets Bigger

 

They start to not worry so much about being perfect or taking up as little space as possible. They try new creative things and feel empowerment in the process.

 

4) Boundaries

 

They are aware of their own energy vs. the energy of others and notice what they need to support themselves in keeping boundaries that will help them.
