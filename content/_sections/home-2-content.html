Lucy Sherman, MS, NCC, CRC

Professional Counselor Associate

EMDR Therapist specializing in adjustment to disability and chronic illness

Welcome 

You may have not always felt heard and seen by the people in your life and it has become harder to express yourself.

 

You may have been given messages that your emotional pain and physical pain were not valid and trusting your intuition and believing in yourself has become difficult.

 

Our Work Together

I want to support you in your journey as you become more self-compassionate and empowered.

 

I want to honor and support you in processing your emotional and physical experiences while creating a place of acceptance, validation and encouragement for your present self to thrive.

 

 

I provide support in these areas:

  • Trauma
  • Chronic Pain
  • Self Esteem
  • Anxiety
  • Adjustment to Disability and Chronic Illness
  • Life Transitions
  • Emotional abuse
  • Depression
  • Complex PTSD
  • negative self talk
  • Relationship Issues
  • Sexual Abuse
  • Stress
  • Women's issues
  • Teen Issues
  • HSP (Highly Sensitive Person)

Approaches I draw from:

  • Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR)

  • Compassion-Focused Therapy
  • Attachment-Focused Therapy
  • Person-Centered Therapy
