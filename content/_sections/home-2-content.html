Welcome and I appreciate you being here!

Clients I work with often resonate with these sentiments: 

 

 

 

"I have experienced trauma in my life and it has become really hard to trust myself."

 

 

 

"I feel like I should be over things that happened but my body keeps responding as if it is still happening and these things in my past still affect me."

 

 

"I am a people-pleaser and put others' needs before my own. I have trouble making decisions, setting boundaries or knowing what I like."

 

 

"I’m wanting a kind of therapy that involves more than just talk, sometimes I don’t have the words for what I’m really feeling."

 

Lucy Sherman, MS, NCC, CRC

Professional Counselor Associate

She/Her

EMDR Therapist specialized in working with complex trauma

 

A Space for You

 

We create a validating space that honors all of you while I guide you in healing from the trauma you have gone through.

 

The space we build together invites imagination, playfulness and openness that has been hard to access. 

 

We experiment together and create a comforting tool kit that can help you strengthen your relationship with your intuition and trust in self so you can feel authentic and empowered.

 

I have a special passion for working with self-identified women and non-binary people between 20 and 35.
