Welcome 

You may have not always felt heard and validated by the people in your life and it has become harder to share your whole self with others now.

 

You may have been given messages that your emotional pain and physical pain are not real and trusting your intuition and believing in yourself has gotten more difficult.

 

Our Work Together

I want to honor what your past self has gone through while creating a place of acceptance, validation and encouragement for your present self to thrive. I am passionate about helping you connect with the empowered you that feels just out of reach so you can move toward the life that you want.

I provide support in these areas:

  • Trauma
  • Chronic Pain
  • Self Esteem
  • Anxiety
  • Adjustment to Disability and Chronic Illness
  • Life Transitions
  • Emotional abuse
  • Depression
  • Complex PTSD
  • negative self talk
  • Relationship Issues
  • Sexual Abuse
  • Stress
  • Women's issues
  • Teen Issues
