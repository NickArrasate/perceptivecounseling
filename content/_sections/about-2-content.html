Lucy Sherman, MS, CRC, NCC, LPC Intern

I am honored to help you explore the changes you want in your life as you hone in on how you can gain more self compassion and empowerment.

It is with great joy that I get to bring authenticity, empathy, curiosity, presence and humor into the counseling room.

Our therapeutic alliance is one of the most crucial elements for creating the positive changes you want in your life, therefore finding the right fit is key.

I believe that every person has the capacity to foster self-compassion and self-healing. Therapy offers a space for you to gain insight into how you can support all of who you are.

As a somatic-attachment focused EMDR therapist, I work with you to delve into how your experiences, memories, and ways you have learned to adapt impact your life and affect your current relationship with yourself and others.

As a Clinical Rehabilitation Counselor with a speciality in adjustment to disability and chronic illness, I understand how both physical and emotional experiences can influence the way we live our lives and how we see ourselves. I work holistically to honor your physiological and emotional responses to stress.

I consider nonverbal communication to be just as valuable as verbal communication, therefore I draw from both nonverbal and verbal communication within the therapy room.
