Lucy Sherman, MS, NCC, CRC, LPC Intern

It is with great joy that I get to bring authenticity, empathy, curiosity, presence, humor, and creativity into the counseling room. 

I work with adults and teens from a non-judgmental framework where you are the expert on your own life. I am honored to collaborate together and form a relationship where you can feel comfortable, seen and heard while sharing the parts of you that have been hidden, dismissed or overlooked. I want to appreciate and validate your strengths and hone in on how you can be the you that you want to be in the life that you hope for. In our counseling relationship, I strive to create a warm space where you can explore both their physical and emotional experiences openly. I work relationally and draw from strength-based, compassion-focused therapy, mindfulness approaches, and EMDR.
